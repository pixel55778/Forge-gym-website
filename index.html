Why we cook this way

Live fire doesn't forgive shortcuts.

We grew up around tandoors and roadside grills, where the fire told you everything — too hot, too cold, ready, not ready. That discipline is the whole menu.

No gas burners, no microwaves. Every dish passes through real coal, which means every plate takes longer and tastes like it.
